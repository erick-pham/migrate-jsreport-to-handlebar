What is HappiU?

HappiU is your future financial satisfaction. The higher the score, the more likely you will achieve your self-defined financial needs and aspirations while maintaining your current lifestyle. We even stress test your future over 10,000 personal and market risk scenarios, so that your HappiU score reflects your future reality.
